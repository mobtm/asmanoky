BALKAN SHIPYARDS

Located in Turkmenbashi, Balkan Shipbuilding and Repairing Yard is the first step in the field of shipbuilding and repairing in Turkmenistan. The yard makes a great contribution to the national economy as well as development of maritime and river transportation sphere. We, in our turn, have developed the website of the plant.
Our specialists have developed the website in three languages. The yard is capable of repairing 20-30 ships by processing 2,000 tons of steel annually. The important information, such as services, contacts, images, history and short introduction of the yard is shown in numbers.
